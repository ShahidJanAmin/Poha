Observatipon of binary fission in amoeba by using slides or charts

Instruction

  1. Select Dropper and take 1 drop from 0.1 Sugar solution beaker and drop it on X slide.
  2. Select Dropper and take 1 drop from 0.9 Sugar solution beaker and drop it on Y slide.
  3. Select Dropper and take 1 drop from 5 Sugar solution beaker and drop it on Z slide.
  4. Cover the slides.
  5. Put X slide on microscope and observe. Press back to stop observing.
  6. Put Y slide on microscope and observe. Press back to stop observing.
  7. Put Z slide on microscope and observe. Press back to stop observing.
