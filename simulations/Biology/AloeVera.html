AloeVera Simulation

Instruction

  1. Take one AloeVera piece and cut it from the middle with the help of blade.
  2. Pick a small piece of Aloevera with forceps and put it on the glass slide.
  3. Add few drops of water and Eosin solution with the help of dropper.
  4. Place glassslide in Microscope and observe Cell Wall, Cell Membrance, Cytoplasam, Nucleas and Chloroplast.
